How The System Works

1. Capture Cameras, phone photos, barcode scans, work notes, and technician updates enter the system.
2. Understand Vision reads labels, identifies parts, detects shelf changes, and connects photos to jobs.
3. Track Jobs, inventory, labor, approvals, estimates, invoices, and vehicle history stay linked.
4. Communicate Enrique drafts customer texts, emails, estimates, and job summaries for approval.
5. Improve The shop gets low-stock alerts, follow-up reminders, daily briefs, and video clip suggestions.
